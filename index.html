Volatile Legacy

Storyline

You crash-landed at a watchtower, where you were lucky to survive — but your balloon is broken. After arriving at the hospital, you almost wonder how you got there. In fact, you forget what brought you there.

In this old world with new tech sort of universe, you get to do most things that you do in other RPGs. Bringing the natural world to your fingertips, and hoping it brings value as you can retain and carry over your held essentials to another decentralized space on Base or Solana.

Message from the developer:

My name is Mike. Welcome to this Ecosystem, Volatile Legacy. I always wanted to make a game, but never had the tools until now to pursue a dream like this.

I've tried writing down a game onto paper, coding it myself with just tablets and regular phones, using apps to simulate coding. It wasn't ever going to be enough. Computer and 3 screens was the way.

Well now I'm ready and doing it, and proud to bring to you this hopeful store of value. I want everyone to be involved, and have fun. That brings me to my real mission.

The mission goal is to generate an Ecosystem where basically any income level can participate. I'd like anyone who may not fully grasp the simplicity of tokenization of their gaming progress to finally see what not enough people have found in the DeFi space.

Base and Solana. If you join us, you will quickly become familiar with these names. These go hand in hand with the Volatile Legacy Ecosystem. Transactions on Base, HODL on SOL. Simplenomics.

VLChronicles

Welcome to the past. This is where all in-game stories that are yet to be encountered. A sneak peek into the future.

You can easily unlock these by simply holding their relative tokens. Each token acts sort of like an NFT in this case as a key.

Any amount held per Token is perfectly acceptable.

(Hint: have a second wallet with a set of the tokens to open a second information window).


A lot of my inspiration dates back to when I was a young person and my dad allowed me to play video games when others didn't want me to. I got to experience Final Fantasy 1. It was so hard for me to figure out at like 5 or 6 when it was my first game ever playing. One day I'd know how big of a deal Final Fantasy would be to me. The best gaming franchise and developmental storyline ever generated, in my opinion.

A lot of the solidified battle mechanics are not directly based on Final Fantasy, but the number systems are inspired by the creators. I've played all up to 12 except I think 3 and 5 or 6. I didn't have time to dive into one or the other — they came in a multi disc case. As well as Elder Scrolls which came later in my life in the form of Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind. This was followed by 4 and 5. Stopped there. Too much money to keep up with new systems.

The music is a mix of both Elder Scrolls and Final Fantasy. I'm listening to the Final Fantasy XI soundtrack right now as I type this and sort of tearing up thinking about how long I've waited to build a video game. I hope I make something great.

I believe mobile gaming is the only future that's sensible for gaming now. As well as play to own.

🏦 Banking Info

The Bank is a place to go when you want to forget you have money. Aaand it's gone!

Watches, Deeds, Gold Bonuses. These are just some of the things that only the most practiced Banking Master could meaningfully wield. Not everyone knows who Mohs is.

Not everyone can fit a Diamond on a scale (mostly since they don't got a Diamond to put on the scale).

Banking Mastery Bonus per level =
(% of Banking Mastery Tokens in wallet at the time of Action) × [(current constant of the event) + (current NFT equipment bonuses)]

📱 Intel Info

Knowledge is worth a lot and so is Mastery. It's almost the same one from the other but it's really about who you know.

Cell Phone, Mayor's Note, Binoculars. These are some of the Equipment related to Intel Mastery.

🌿 Gathering Info

Where there's a stick, there might also be a shroom silently growing under it. Gather 1 but not the other if you aren't a Master.

Hands, Shovel, Net, Gold Pan. These are some of the Tools associated with Gathering Mastery.

Gathering Mastery Bonus:
(% of Gathering Mastery Tokens in wallet at the time of Action) × [(current constant of the event) + (current NFT equipment bonuses)]

🎣 Fishing Info

There's a hook for every pull. Holding is the rule.

Fishing Rod, Pluck, Slots. Good luck can cure Sickness, and a good Pluck. Cheers.

🍳 Cooking Info

Too good is what one might say after that 1/3 good batches of macaroni and cheese. Not every meal is perfect except for when you know what you're doing.

Pan, Golden Spatula, Pot. Those are some of the tools a Master Cook will use throughout their practice.
